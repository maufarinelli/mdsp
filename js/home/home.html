Welcome to Morro de São Paulo.

Morro de São Paulo is one of those places people just want to stay forever. It has 4 beautiful beaches with crystal clear water amidst the coral reefs which delights any visitor.

Located in Tinharé island, south of Salvador, capital of Bahia\'s Province, it is blessed by Nature and has a welcoming village inviting you to go for a walk any time of the day.

There is no cars in the island, but believe me, you won\'t miss your vehicle if you like to walk, breathe fresh air and feel that pleasant smell of a tropical beach.

Are you looking for a beatiful and very quiet beach? Morro de São Paulo has it! Do you want a busier beach and party? Morro de São Paulo has too. Do you prefer to walk by the sea? Or to swim a bit and stay afloat for hours? Morro de São Paulo is a place for everyone.

Nightlife has also a lot of options. You can find party on the nightclubs or romantic moments in some restaurants with some live music. You can find bars to have some cold beers with your friends or restaurants for families with kids. Restaurants facing the sea or in the middle of the village. Morro de São Paulo has absolutely everything. In fact, food is one of the strengths of Morro de São Paulo.

In addition to the beaches you must go, Morro de São Paulo has several tours and activities to do. Go up on the lighthouse hill to admire the view and go down the tyrolean traverse, take a boat ride around the entire island, diving or snorkeling, see the sunset in the fortress, surf in the first beach and much more.
